One platform, four jobs done

Run the whole operation, not four disconnected tools

Most cafés stitch together a spreadsheet, a WhatsApp group, a payroll guy and a shoebox of receipts. Bashari replaces all of it.

01

People & payroll

From the first clock-in to the monthly slip. Attendance per branch, leave approvals, salary advances, warnings, documents — and payroll that pulls holiday overtime straight from the timesheet.

  • Branch attendance
  • Leave & approvals
  • Payroll runs
  • 1.5× holiday OT
  • Advances
  • Warnings
  • Certificates
02

Daily operations

Opening and closing checklists per branch and per role, daily & weekly counts, and expiry checks that won't let a fridge slip. Every shift leaves a record.

  • Branch checklists
  • Daily count
  • Weekly count
  • Expiry checklist
  • Task board
  • Roasting log
03

Inventory & spend

Stock movements, suppliers and bakery purchasing in one ledger. Snap a receipt and AI reads the vendor, total and VAT for you — then it lands in the branch spend report.

  • Stock & movements
  • Suppliers
  • Bakery stock
  • Purchases
  • AI receipt scan
  • Spend reports
04

Hiring, end to end

A public application page you share on Instagram or a QR code at the counter. Applications flow into a pipeline — review, interview, hire — and a hired applicant becomes an employee in one click.

  • Public apply form
  • CV uploads
  • Pipeline stages
  • Bilingual
  • One-click hire
  • PDPL-aware
